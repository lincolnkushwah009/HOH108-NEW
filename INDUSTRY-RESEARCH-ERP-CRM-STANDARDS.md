## 1. INTERIOR DESIGN INDUSTRY ERP/CRM STANDARDS

### 1.1 Leading Platform Feature Comparison

#### Houzz Pro (absorbed Ivy in 2024)
- **CRM & Lead Management**: Full customer lifecycle tracking, lead capture, email marketing
- **Design Tools**: Mood boards, 3D floor plans, AR tours, product libraries
- **Project Management**: Scheduling, task management, daily logs, procurement tracking
- **Financial Tools**: Estimates, proposals, invoicing, online payments, change orders, financial reports
- **Client Portal**: Client dashboard with project visibility
- **Integrations**: QuickBooks sync
- **Marketing**: Custom websites, targeted ads, email campaigns, lead generation
- **Pricing**: Essentials $99/mo, Pro $159/mo (billed annually), additional users $60/mo each

#### Studio Designer (absorbed Mydoma in July 2024)
- **Accounting Suite**: Full AR/AP, balance sheets, income statements, cash flow
- **Procurement**: Vendor management, purchase orders, order tracking, specifications
- **Proposals & Invoicing**: Client-facing proposals, invoicing, Studio Pay
- **Dashboards & Reporting**: Advanced financial reporting, profitability tracking
- **Client Portal**: Approval workflows, payment management
- **Product Library**: Centralized product/vendor database
- **Pricing**: Essentials $69/mo, Enterprise $79/mo, Premier $99/mo

#### Design Manager
- **Core Strength**: Accounting and purchasing (legacy platform, deepest financial tools)
- **Accounting**: Retainer tracking, deposits, invoices, payments, income statements, balance sheets, cash flow
- **Purchasing**: Purchase/work orders synced to projects and accounting
- **Specifications**: Carry design decisions into purchasing/billing automatically
- **Reporting**: Deposit/retainer analysis, firm-level profitability
- **Weakness**: Reporting is limited compared to modern platforms; inflexible workflows

#### DesignFiles
- **Target**: Independent/small designers, e-design workflows
- **Web Clipper**: One-click product saving from any website with auto spec filling
- **Product Library**: Organize/reuse items across projects
- **Procurement**: Request quotes, create POs, track orders
- **FF&E Schedules**: Auto-generated spec sheets with real-time updates
- **Client Collaboration**: Shared boards, approvals
- **Weakness**: Lacks advanced procurement controls, multi-project management, tied scheduling

#### Programa
- **Full Workflow Coverage**: Specification, procurement, delivery, task management
- **Web Clipper**: Import specs/images from suppliers into centralized product library
- **Procurement Hub**: Manage payments and deliveries directly from schedules
- **Purchase Orders**: Fully integrated PO generation
- **Client Communication**: Streamlined client interaction features
- **Rating**: 4.8 stars on Capterra

### 1.2 Standard Feature Requirements for Interior Design ERP/CRM

| Category | Required Features |
|----------|------------------|
| **CRM** | Lead capture, pipeline management, client profiles, communication history, follow-up reminders |
| **Design Management** | Mood boards, product libraries, specifications, FF&E schedules |
| **Project Management** | Phase tracking, task assignment, timeline/Gantt, milestones, dependencies |
| **Procurement** | Vendor database, purchase orders, order tracking, delivery management, returns |
| **Financial** | Estimates/proposals, invoicing, payments, retainer tracking, AR/AP, profitability reports |
| **Client Portal** | Project status, approvals, document access, payment portal, messaging |
| **Reporting** | Project profitability, firm financials, utilization, pipeline analytics |

### 1.3 Standard Interior Design Project Workflow (7 Stages)

1. **Discovery & Intake** - Lead capture, initial consultation, requirement gathering
2. **Concept Development** - Mood boards, concept presentations, space planning
3. **Design Development** - Detailed designs, material selection, specifications
4. **Documentation & Estimation** - BOQ, cost estimation, proposals, contracts
5. **Procurement & Project Management** - PO generation, vendor coordination, order tracking, site management
6. **Installation & Styling** - On-site coordination, furniture placement, quality checks, punch/snag lists
7. **Handover & Closeout** - Final walkthrough, documentation, warranties, maintenance guides, feedback

## 2. CONSTRUCTION ERP BEST PRACTICES

### 2.1 Leading Platform Feature Comparison

#### Procore (Enterprise/Commercial)
- **Preconstruction**: Prequalification, bid management
- **Project Management**: Scheduling, task management, daily logs, RFIs, submittals, drawings
- **Quality & Safety**: Inspections, observations, safety incidents, compliance tracking
- **Financial Management**: Budget tracking (original + changes), commitment tracking (subcontracts/POs), budget forecasting, WIP reporting, invoice management, lien waivers, change order management

- **Analytics**: 360 Reporting with tax data integration, equipment telematics

- **CRM**: Lead tracking, proposals
- **Project Management**: Scheduling, daily logs, to-dos, selections
- **Financial**: Estimates, change orders, invoicing, QuickBooks/Xero sync
- **Client Portal**: Real-time updates, selections, approvals

| **User Access Reviews** | Periodic access reviews, certification of user privileges |

## 6. DATA PRIVACY - INDIA DPDP ACT

### 6.1 Compliance Timeline
- **November 13, 2025**: Data Protection Board established, administrative provisions
- **November 13, 2026**: Consent Manager registration deadline
- **May 13, 2027**: Full substantive compliance deadline

### 6.2 Mandatory Features

| Requirement | ERP Feature Required |
|-------------|---------------------|
| **Consent Management** | Separate, clear consent notices; granular consent collection; consent withdrawal mechanism |
| **Data Encryption** | Encryption at rest and in transit for all personal data |
| **Access Control** | Role-based access, access logging, monitoring |
| **Data Masking** | PII masking in non-production environments, display masking for sensitive fields |
| **Breach Notification** | 72-hour breach notification capability to Data Protection Board and affected individuals |
| **Data Subject Rights** | Access, correction, erasure mechanisms; grievance redressal; nominee appointment |
| **Data Backup** | Automated backups with encryption |
| **Unauthorized Access Detection** | Intrusion detection, anomaly monitoring |
| **Data Retention Policy** | Configurable retention periods, automated deletion/anonymization |
| **Purpose Limitation** | Track and enforce data processing purposes |
| **Children's Data** | Verifiable parental consent for processing children's data |

### 6.3 Significant Data Fiduciary (SDF) Enhanced Obligations
- Annual Data Protection Impact Assessments (DPIAs)
- Annual audits by independent auditors
- Algorithmic fairness assessments
- Appointment of Data Protection Officer (DPO)
- Stricter technical due diligence

### 6.4 Penalties
- Failure to maintain security safeguards: Up to **Rs 250 crore**
- Failure to notify breach: Up to **Rs 200 crore**
- Violations relating to children's data: Up to **Rs 200 crore**
- Other violations: Up to **Rs 50 crore**

## 7. GST COMPLIANCE INDIA

### 7.1 E-Invoicing Requirements (2025-2026)

| Requirement | Detail |
|-------------|--------|
| **Mandatory Threshold** | Turnover > Rs 5 crore (B2B transactions) |
| **30-Day Reporting (Apr 2025)** | Businesses with AATO > Rs 10 crore must report B2B e-invoices to IRP within 30 days |
| **2FA Requirement (Apr 2025)** | All taxpayers must use two-factor authentication for e-invoice/e-way bill generation |
| **IRP Integration** | Must include seller/buyer GSTIN, item description, HSN/SAC, value, tax breakup, place of supply |
| **SME Expansion (Oct 2025)** | Rs 2 crore turnover threshold brings more SMEs under mandatory e-invoicing |

### 7.2 HSN/SAC Code Compliance

| Code | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **9954** | Construction services (building and construction work) |
| **99541-99542** | Construction services for buildings (apartments, offices, factories) |
| **9983** | Other professional, technical, and business services (interior design consulting) |
| **Penalty** | Rs 50/day for failure to mention HSN/SAC code, up to Rs 5,000 max |

### 7.3 E-Way Bill Requirements
- Required for movement of goods exceeding Rs 50,000
- E-Way Bill validity extensions capped (Apr 2025 bill valid until Mar 2026)
- Integration with e-invoicing system for seamless generation

### 7.4 GST Filing Features Required

| Feature | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **GSTR-1 Preparation** | Auto-populate from e-invoices and sales data |
| **GSTR-3B Filing** | Summary return with tax liability and ITC claims |
| **ITC Reconciliation** | Match GSTR-2A/2B with purchase records for ITC claims |
| **HSN/SAC Mapping** | Product/service to HSN/SAC code mapping with rate lookup |
| **Multi-GST Registration** | Support for multiple GSTINs under one PAN |
| **ISD Registration (Apr 2025)** | Input Service Distributor for businesses with multiple GSTINs |
| **TDS/TCS Compliance** | TDS deduction tracking (TCS on sale of goods removed Apr 2025) |
| **GST Reconciliation Reports** | GSTR-1 vs books, GSTR-2A vs books, GSTR-3B vs books |
| **E-Invoice Generation** | JSON/API integration with IRP (Invoice Registration Portal) |
| **E-Way Bill Generation** | Auto-generation from invoices with goods movement details |
| **GSP Integration** | Connect via GST Suvidha Provider for filing |
| **Credit/Debit Notes** | GST-compliant credit/debit note generation |

### 7.5 Exemptions
- SEZ units exempt from mandatory e-invoicing
- Financial institutions and insurance providers exempt from IRN generation
- Goods Transport Agencies and passenger transport services excluded

## 8. INDUSTRY KPIs

### 8.1 Financial KPIs

| KPI | Benchmark | Description |
|-----|-----------|-------------|
| **Gross Profit Margin** | 35-55% (design), 40-70% (professional services) | Revenue minus direct costs / Revenue |
| **Net Profit Margin** | 15-20% | After all expenses |
| **Project Profitability** | 20-30% minimum per project | Revenue vs. all project costs |
| **Overhead Rate** | <=175% of direct costs | Total overhead / Total direct costs |
| **Revenue per Employee** | Varies by market | Total revenue / Full-time employees |
| **Accounts Receivable Days** | <45 days | Average time to collect payment |
| **Cash Flow** | Positive monthly | Net cash in vs. cash out |
| **Markup on Goods** | 20-35% on wholesale | Common cost-plus model (used by 60%+ of high-earning firms) |

### 8.2 Operational KPIs

| KPI | Benchmark | Description |
|-----|-----------|-------------|
| **Utilization Rate** | 65-80% | Billable hours / Total hours worked |
| **Project Timeline Variance** | <=10-15% | Actual vs. planned timeline deviation |
| **Budget Variance** | <=10% | Actual vs. estimated cost deviation |
| **On-Time Project Delivery** | >85% | Projects delivered on or before deadline |
| **Change Order Frequency** | Track trend | Number of change orders per project |
| **Procurement Lead Time** | Track trend | Order placement to delivery |
| **Punch List Items** | Decreasing trend | Snag items per project at handover |

### 8.3 Client KPIs

| KPI | Benchmark | Description |
|-----|-----------|-------------|
| **Client Satisfaction Score** | 75-85% | Post-project survey scores |
| **Client Retention Rate** | >70% | Repeat clients / Total clients |
| **Net Promoter Score (NPS)** | >50 | Likelihood to recommend |
| **Referral Rate** | >30% | New clients from referrals |
| **Response Time** | <24 hours | Average time to respond to client queries |

### 8.4 Sales/Pipeline KPIs

| KPI | Benchmark | Description |
|-----|-----------|-------------|
| **Lead-to-Client Conversion** | 15-25% | Leads converted to paying clients |
| **Average Deal Size** | Track trend | Average project value |
| **Pipeline Value** | 3-6x monthly revenue | Total value of active opportunities |
| **Sales Cycle Length** | Track trend | Lead to signed contract duration |
| **Proposal Win Rate** | >40% | Proposals accepted / Proposals sent |
